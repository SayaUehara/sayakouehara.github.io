hi

I am a PhD candidate in linguistics at Michigan State University. My co-advisors are Yen-Hwei Lin and Karthik Durvasula. My committee members also include Suzanne Evans Wagner, Cristina Schmitt, and Mutsuko Endo Hudson. I am also part of the MSU Phonology & Phonetics Group and the MSU Sociolinguistics Lab.

Please contact me for my up-to-date CV.

research

My research interests revolve around phonology, phonetics, and sociolinguistics. Most recently, I have been studying the possible cues to word segmentation for adult speakers, looking at both language specific (experience based) and language independent (experience independent) cues.

I have also investigated the effect of vocalic outliers on perception for one of my qualifying papers.
